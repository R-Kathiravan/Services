1. Personal Requirements

Before we look at your car, let's make sure you meet the baseline requirements to become a partner.

Age & Experience

You must be at least 21 years old and have a minimum of 1 year of licensed driving experience in your city.

Background Check

All partners must pass a thorough screening, including a review of your driving record and criminal history.

Smartphone

You need a reliable device to run the partner app. iPhone 8 or newer, or an Android running version 9.0+.

Bank Account

An active, local bank account in your name is required to receive your daily or weekly instant payouts.

2. Ride Tiers & Upgrades

Unlock higher earning potential by qualifying for premium ride tiers. Your vehicle class determines your payout rates.

Stackly Economy

Standard Rates

The everyday ride for the majority of our users. High volume, consistent trips.

  • Standard hatchbacks & sedans
  • Seats 4 passengers
  • E.g., Swift, i20, Amaze

Stackly Premium

+40% Earnings

High-end vehicles for business travelers and special nights out. Requires a 4.85+ driver rating.

  • Mid-to-full size luxury sedans
  • Leather interiors required
  • E.g., Honda City, Skoda Slavia

Stackly XL

+60% Earnings

Large vehicles for groups and airport runs with luggage.

  • Minivans & SUVs
  • Seats 6+ passengers comfortably
  • E.g., Toyota Innova, Ertiga
