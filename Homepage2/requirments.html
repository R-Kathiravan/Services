1. Personal Requirements

Before we look at your car, let's make sure you meet the baseline requirements to become a partner.

Age & Experience

You must be at least 21 years old and have a minimum of 1 year of licensed driving experience in your city.

Background Check

All partners must pass a thorough screening, including a review of your driving record and criminal history.

Smartphone

You need a reliable device to run the partner app. iPhone 8 or newer, or an Android running version 9.0+.

Bank Account

An active, local bank account in your name is required to receive your daily or weekly instant payouts.

4. Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from new partners. Didn't find your answer?

Can I use someone else's car?

+

Yes! The vehicle doesn't need to be in your name, but you must have written permission from the owner and all required documents (RC, insurance).

What's the approval timeline?

+

95% of complete applications approved within 24 hours. Upload clear photos and we'll get you on the road fast.

Do I need commercial license?

+

No private light motor vehicle license is fine. Commercial endorsement required only for heavy vehicles.

Are there model year limits?

+

Economy: 2015+, Premium: 2020+, XL: No limit if maintained well. Always condition > age.

How often vehicle inspections?

+

Monthly self-checklist + random spot inspections. Major 50-point every 6 months free of charge.

Can I switch ride tiers?

+

Yes! Upgrade anytime with vehicle change + rating requirement. Downgrades instant.

Pet policy for vehicles?

+

No permanent pet damage. Seat covers recommended. Clean between rides required.

What's rejected most often?

+

1. Blurry document photos (30%), 2. Expired insurance (25%), 3. Dirty vehicles (20%).
