Quick Help

Find answers to the most common questions

Don't worry! Here's what you can do:

1. Open your Stackly app and go to "Your Trips"

2. Select the trip where you lost your item

3. Tap "Lost Item" and select "Contact Driver"

4. Our team will help coordinate with your driver to retrieve your item

Note: Please try to contact us within 30 days of your trip. Drivers may charge a small fee for returning items.

Drivers receive weekly payouts every Monday for the previous week's earnings. If Monday falls on a holiday, payouts are processed on the next business day.

You can also enable "Instant Cash Out" to withdraw your earnings instantly to your bank account (available in select cities). There may be a small fee for instant withdrawals.

To cancel a ride:

1. Open the Stackly app

2. Tap on your upcoming trip

3. Tap "Cancel Trip"

Note: A cancellation fee may apply if you cancel after the driver has arrived or after the free cancellation window (2 minutes) has passed.

Join our driver community in just a few steps:

1. Download the "Stackly Driver" app

2. Create your account and submit required documents

3. Complete our background check process

4. Attend a brief orientation session

Requirements: Valid driver's license, vehicle inspection, clean background check, and meet minimum age requirements (21 in most cities).

Our fare estimates are calculated based on:

• Base fare (pickup fee)

• Time and distance of the trip

• Current demand in your area (surge pricing)

• Applicable tolls and fees

The final fare may vary slightly based on actual conditions during your trip. You'll always see the estimated fare before confirming your ride.
